Privacy policy

Like millions of other website owners out there, we use Google Analytics on yorkcs.com / jaredyork.com (jaredyork.github.io).

Google Analytics is a software that collects anonymous non-identifying information about our visitors.


What does Google Analytics collect?

  • What website you came from to get here.
  • How long you stay on the site.
  • What kind of device you're using.
  • And quite a bit more.

What do you do with this information?

The information collected helps us understand what kind of people are visiting. The information also helps with letting us know what devices we may need to adjust the display of the website for so you can get the best possible experience on our site.

Occasionally we compile statistics about the number of visitors this site receives and browsers being used. No personally identifiable data is included in these statistics.


Want to opt out of tracking?

You can use a browser plugin to opt out of all Google Analytics tracking software.
