Swim Penguin

Swim Penguin is a little retro adventure game where you help a cute lil' penguin swim between as many ice columns as possible.

How to play

  • Tap the screen to make your penguin move
  • Swim through the gaps in the ice columns and avoid obstacles
  • Help guide your penguin and gain bragging rights among your friends!


Features

  • A new procedurally generated underwater adventure each time you play
  • Challenging ice spikes and ice cubes to be mindful of along the way
  • Game Center integration with a leaderboard (for bragging rights, of course)
